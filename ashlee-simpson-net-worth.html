    What is Ashlee Simpson’s Net Worth?

    Ashlee Simpson, younger sister of pop star Jessica Simpson, has a net worth of $11 million.

    She is an American singer-songwriter and actress who has managed to establish her own career in pop music and reality television.

    Despite her famous sibling, Ashlee has been successful in carving out her own path in the entertainment industry.

    Ashlee Simpson’s Early Life and Career

    Ashlee Simpson was born on October 3rd, 1984, in Waco, Texas. She moved to Los Angeles in 1999, alongside her family, as her father Joe Simpson focused on her older sister Jessica’s pop career. Simpson started her career as a TV commercial actress and backup dancer for her sister’s concerts. Later, she pursued a higher-profile music career of her own while continuing to work as a TV commercial actress.

    Ashlee Simpson’s Career Highlights

    Ashlee Simpson started her career with minor acting roles in TV shows and movies like Malcolm in the Middle and The Hot Chick. She gained popularity as a series regular in 7th Heaven, but her interest in music led her to release her first song “Christmas Past, Present, and Future” in 2002. Her breakthrough in the music industry came with her debut album Autobiography, released in 2004, which positioned her as an edgier alternative to her sister Jessica by incorporating a rock-influenced sound.

    However, her fame took a hit in 2004 when she lip-synched on an episode of Saturday Night Live. The incident got a significant amount of media attention and mockery, and her next single “Shadow” failed to match the commercial success of her previous releases. She released her second album I Am Me in 2005, which sold well but failed to match the success of its predecessor. Her third album Bittersweet World, released in 2008, marked a stylistic departure from her previous efforts and continued her commercial decline.

    Ashlee Simpson returned to TV with a regular role on the CW’s Melrose Place revival in 2009 and appeared as a guest judge on America’s Next Top Model, Cycle 17 in 2011. After some time out of the spotlight, she returned to reality TV in 2018 with her husband Evan Ross and released a single together entitled “I Do.”

    Ashlee Simpson’s Success on the Musical Stage

    Ashlee Simpson found success on the musical stage, starting in 2009 when she played Roxie Hart in the Broadway and London productions of the musical Chicago. She received positive reviews for her performance, and in 2013 she reprised the role in the Hollywood Bowl production of Chicago. Critics described her as “an audience favorite” in this production.

    Ashlee Simpson’s Marriages and Children

    Ashlee Simpson married Pete Wentz, the bassist of Fall Out Boy, in 2008. The couple had one child, Bronx Mowgli, before divorcing in 2011. In 2014, Simpson got married again, this time to Evan Ross, son of Diana Ross. They welcomed their first child, Jagger Ross, in 2015. In April 2020, the couple announced Simpson’s second pregnancy on Instagram.

    Ashlee Simpson and Evan Ross’ Homes

    Ashlee Simpson and Evan Ross’s Los Angeles home was featured on their reality show “Ashlee + Evan.” The house is a creative haven for the couple to pursue their artistic interests and reportedly inspired by the Gramercy Park Hotel in New York City. In September 2020, they purchased a 6,000 square-foot mansion in Encino, California for $4.5 million.
