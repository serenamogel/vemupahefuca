    The Bachelor premieres a new season on ABC on Monday, January 23 at 8 p.m. ET.

    Season 27 of the popular dating show will feature 30 women competing for the love and adoration of Zach Shallcross, who was last seen on The Bachelorette season 19 but did not win Rachel Recchia’s heart. Holland Parsons of Boca Raton, Florida, is one of the contenders who will try to develop a close relationship with Zach and may propose to him before the season ends.

    Bachelor contestant from Season 27 Holland Parsons is a cutting-edge insurance marketer

    According to LinkedIn, Holland, 24, graduated from Clemson University and was a member of the Alpha Chi Omega sorority. She holds a Bachelor of Science in Tourism and Travel Services Management as well as an insurance representative license from FLDFS.

    She interned as an Assistant Event Manager at Auberge du Soleil in 2019. Parsons also worked as a Financial Operations Manager at Keller Williams Realty Boca Raton for a year. She recently left her part-time executive assistant position at American Eagle Ventures LLC after nearly three years of service in December 2022.

    Holland describes herself as a “spunky and confident lady” looking for “unwavering devotion, loyalty, and a true personality” in a partner. According to her ABC profile, Holland enjoys wine, sushi, and sailing with her girlfriends. Her favorite television show is The Bachelor, and she enjoys Pilates and going to the beach in her home state of Florida. Furthermore, the description states:

    “Holland knows what she wants; good luck stopping her!”

    She can’t even “function” in the absence of air conditioning. Parsons currently has over 1800 Instagram followers, and she enjoys spending time on boats, according to her posts.

    More details on The Bachelor season 27

    Zach Shallcross left The Bachelorette season 19 after realizing his connection with Rachel Recchia was not as strong as he had hoped. He was named the show’s next lead on September 20, 2022, following the conclusion of The Bachelorette.

    After completing several tasks and going on dates with the 30 hopefuls, he will now try to connect with them and, hopefully, propose to one of them in the finale. ABC describes the series as follows:

    “After a painful exit from Gabby and Rachel’s season of “The Bachelorette,” Zach Shallcross’ adventure to find love will continue as he steps in as leading man for the upcoming 27th season of “The Bachelor.”

    Additionally, it states:

    “Shallcross captured Bachelor Nation’s heart when he made the difficult decision to end his journey on “The Bachelorette” following an emotional realization about his future with Rachel.

    “The charming California native will begin giving out roses when the season debuts.”

    Jesse Palmer will host Season 27 of The Bachelor, which is currently in production in Los Angeles. Aside from that, the exhibition will include Tallinn, The Bahamas, and Hungary. On Monday, January 23, ABC will air the season premiere of The Bachelor to see if Holland can make a good first impression on Zach.
