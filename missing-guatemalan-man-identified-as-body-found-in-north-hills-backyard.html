    Murder Investigation in North Hills, California

    A chilling murder investigation has unfolded in the quiet neighborhood of North Hills, California. The community was left in shock when homicide detectives and L.A. city fire crews arrived at the crime scene on Community Court. The incident, which took place on November 21, 2023, has sent shockwaves through the community, leaving residents concerned for their safety.

    Arrest and Charges

    In a significant breakthrough, a North Hills man has been apprehended and is now being held without bail on a murder charge. The suspect, identified as Russell Robinson, 48, was arrested in connection with the disappearance and subsequent death of Carlos DeLeon, a 35-year-old Guatemalan man. The arrest has brought some relief to the victim’s family and friends, who have been anxiously awaiting answers about DeLeon’s whereabouts.

    Discovery of the Victim’s Body

    The investigation took a grim turn when authorities received a tip that led them to Robinson’s backyard. After meticulously searching the area, investigators made a horrifying discovery: the buried remains of Carlos DeLeon. The news sent shockwaves through the community, as the reality of the crime hit home. The victim’s family and loved ones are now left to grapple with the devastating loss.

    Police Investigation and Arrest

    The Los Angeles Police Department has been diligently working on this case for several weeks, leaving no stone unturned in their pursuit of justice. The dedicated detectives involved in the investigation have meticulously gathered evidence, interviewed witnesses, and followed leads to ensure a thorough and comprehensive inquiry. Their tireless efforts have culminated in the arrest of Russell Robinson, providing a sense of closure to the community and the victim’s loved ones.

    Suspect’s Bail and Court Date

    Russell Robinson, the prime suspect in this heinous crime, is currently being held at the Valley Jail in Van Nuys. The court has set his bail at a staggering $2 million, reflecting the severity of the charges against him. As of now, no information regarding his court date has been made available. The legal proceedings will undoubtedly be closely watched by the community, as they seek justice for Carlos DeLeon.

    Contact Information for Additional Information

    The authorities are urging anyone with additional information about this incident to come forward and assist in the ongoing investigation. Detective Moselle can be reached at 818-374-9550 for those who wish to provide any relevant details. Alternatively, individuals who prefer to remain anonymous can contact Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-8477 or visit lacrimestoppers.org. Cooperation from the community is crucial in ensuring that justice is served and that no stone is left unturned in this tragic case.
