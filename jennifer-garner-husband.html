    Jennifer Garner husband-American actress, Jennifer Anne Garner was born on April 17, 1972 in Houston, Texas in the United States of America.

    Garner was born to Patricia Ann English and William John Garner. She shares the same parents with her two older sister; Susannah Kay Garner Carpenter and Melissa Garner Wylie.

    According to Garner, she was a normal middle child who tried to set herself apart from her exceptional older sister.

    Garner participated in Vacation Bible School and often attended a nearby United Methodist church. She jokingly remarked that her family’s “take on the world” was “practically Amish” because she and her sisters were forbidden from donning cosmetics, having their ears pierced, painting their nails, or dying their hair as teenagers.

    She went to Charleston’s George Washington High School. Garner enrolled in the Pi Beta Phi sorority and changed her major from chemistry to theater at Denison University in Granville, Ohio, in 1990.

    She attended the National Theater Institute at the Waterford, Connecticut-based Eugene O’Neill Theater Center for the 1993 fall semester.

    She performed in summer stock theater during her undergraduate years. She earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts in theater performing in 1994.

    Jennifer Garner career

    In New York City, Garner started out as an understudy for the Roundabout Theatre Company. In 1995, she made her cinematic debut in Zoya, a television adaptation of Danielle Steel’s romantic novel.

    She starred in the Fox teen drama series Time of Your Life (1999–2000), and she also had supporting parts in Pearl Harbor (2001), a military drama, and Catch Me If You Can (2002), a comedy–drama.

    Garner rose to fame for her role as CIA agent Sydney Bristow in the ABC action-thriller series Alias (2001–2006), for which she received four Primetime Emmy Award nominations, a Golden Globe Award, a SAG Award, and other honors.

    She also played the role of Elektra in the superhero movies Daredevil (2003) and Elektra (2005), as well as the romance comedy 13 Going on 30 (2004).

    The romantic comedies Juno (2007), Ghosts of Girlfriends Past (2009), and Valentine’s Day (2010) have all achieved commercial success.

    Since then, Garner has acted in a number of indie movies, such as the biographical drama Dallas Buyers Club (2013) and the family comedies Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day (2014), Love, Simon (2018), and Yes Day (2021).

    Additionally, she appeared in the action movies The Adam Project (2002) and Peppermint (2018) as well as the AppleTV+ short series The Last Thing He Told Me (2003).

    Garner is a board member of Save the Children USA and a proponent of early childhood education.

    She is a co-founder and the company’s chief brand officer. Once Upon a Farm makes organic baby food. She also supports anti-paparazzi efforts among celebrity kids.

    Who is Jennifer Garner’s husband?

    Jennifer Garner has been married twice and has been in a few relationships. She was previously married to Scott Foley. They got married on October 19, 2000 and divorced in 2004 due to irreconciliable differences.

    Garner also married Hollywood star, Ben Affleck whom she had three children with, two daughters and a son. They got married on June 29, 2005 and divorced in 2018.

    She also had brief relationship Michael Vartan. Garner is currently in a relationship with businessman, John C. Miller.
