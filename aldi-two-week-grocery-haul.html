    I got 54 items at Aldi for $110 - see my shopping list to last two weeks

    FOR those hoping to make their groceries stand the test of time, a TikToker showcased her two week grocery haul in which she only paid $110. She showed the 54 items she purchased at Aldi which she said will last her at least two weeks.

    27 Mar 2024, 00:00

    FOR those hoping to make their groceries stand the test of time, a TikToker showcased her two week grocery haul in which she only paid $110.

    She showed the 54 items she purchased at Aldi which she said will last her at least two weeks.

    “This is like living like royalty grocery haul,” Carissa told her million followers.

    She and her roommate split the grocery bill, which came down to $109.94 altogether.

    It included 54 items in total.

    That was just $55 each for two weeks of groceries per person, a type of affordability that is a major attractor to the bargain grocery brand.

    Here’s what Carissa bought:

    • Clancy’s White Rounds tortilla chips
    • 18 limes
    • Tilapia fish
    • Cilantro

    All of the above were purchased for a Peruvian ceviche dish that Carissa made.

    • Salami
    • Cheese

    Carissa bought those for the arepas she makes in the morning.

    • More sun-dried tomato wraps
    • Chicken cutlets
    • Two tomatoes

    Carissa made sure not to neglect her protein intake during the Aldi trip.

    Included in her haul:

    • Steak

    • Ground turkey

    “Everyone says I look like a vegetarian, but we got steak, salmon, sausage and ground turkey,” Carissa said.

    Carbs also had a place in Carissa’s grocery list.

    • Priano pasta

    • A huge bag of rice

    Lastly, Carissa added

    • Vegetable oil
    • Frost sparkling waters
    • Bananas
    • Refried beans
    • Elbow noodles
    • Greens

    All the groceries last Carissa and one other person around two weeks to get through, at a price of around $50 each.

    Saving at Aldi

    Beyond just taking advantage of the lower prices at Aldi’s, there are several strategies to spend even less at the grocery store.

    First, know the best time for deals.

    Every Tuesday night, Aldi restocks Aldi Finds, which are some of its most popular and heavily discounted products like meals, snacks and desserts.

    By shopping online for pickup or delivery, you also still get access to Aldi’s discounts, but shoppers with the Aldi app can access some extra perks.

    Many cashback and couponing apps also accept Aldi receipts, which opens up an additional avenue for saving money.

    While Aldi has long offered groceries at a lower cost than many competitors, other influencers have made a point to spotlight their top cheap finds at the retailer.

    One superfan shared her five must-haves from the store, including one priced as low as 59 cents.
