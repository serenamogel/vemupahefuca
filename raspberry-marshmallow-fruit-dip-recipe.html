    Raspberry Marshmallow Fruit Dip is an easy to prepare recipe that is sure to be a crowd-pleaser!

    I love serving fresh fruit at parties because it’s a healthy option that tastes great too. And let’s face it – fruit is pretty!  I love all the bright, vibrant colors and there is always something that catches my eye at the store. I made up some dip last weekend for a fruit tray that I had out for the football party. It was so delicious and everyone really enjoyed it. I knew right away that I needed to share it with all of you! {Note: You might want to have a spoon in the dip. Once my boys got their first taste they thought it would be just fine to dip their little fingers right in …}  It’s so easy to pull together and really adds a special touch to the standard fruit tray.

    Raspberry Marshmallow Fruit Dip

    Raspberry Marshmallow Fruit Dip is an easy to prepare recipe that is sure to be a crowd-pleaser!

    Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword raspberry marshmallow fruit dip Prep Time 10 minutes minutes Total Time 10 minutes minutes Servings 4 Calories 326kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 7 oz marshmallow creme 1 jar
    • 8 oz light cream cheese
    • 1/4 cup raspberry preserves or your favorite flavor

    Instructions

    • Beat the cream cheese until nice and fluffy.

    • Add in the marshmallow creme and preserves and beat until well combined.

    • Serve with fresh fruit of your choice and enjoy!
