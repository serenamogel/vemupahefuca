What is multivalued attribute?

Answer It is possible for an attribute to have more than one value at the same time when it is a multivalued attribute. Because a surgeon may possess more than one talent, the skills of a surgeon, for example, are a multivalued characteristic. The address field, which may have numerous values such as zipcode, street address, state, and so on is another prominent example of this kind of field.
