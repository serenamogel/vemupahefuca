    Mystery Energy (Japanese: ミステリーエネルギー Mystery Energy) is a Special Energy card. It was first released as part of the Phantom Forces expansion.

    Card text

    All prints

    Release information

    This card was included in the Phantom Forces expansion, first released in Japan in the Phantom Gate expansion. It was reprinted in the Japanese Premium Trainer Box at the start of the Sun & Moon Era. Despite the layout for Special Energy cards having been changed for the new Era, this card retains its original layout. A reprint later included in the Japanese The Best of XY subset also features the old layout.
