    Personal trainer

    In 2010, Rory hired Steve McGregor to improve his fitness and take the final step toward golf fame. Steve initially focused on closing the gap between his strong right flank and his weaker left flank. Therefore, his training was dominated by a lot of one-armed dumbbells.

    Particular attention was also paid to making the lower body stronger, which helped him inject explosive power into his swings. It also made him more stable while hitting the balls over long distances. He also worked on his core for the same goal.You can watch his example workout in the video below, made in collaboration with Nike.

    Steve also got him to flip his unhealthy eating habits for more disciplined eating habits. He was asked to eat lots of chicken and broccoli to fuel his weight loss program, and he went from 22 percent to 16 percent body fat.

    Rory McIlroy Facts

  • Rory was introduced to golf at a young age by his father, who was himself an amateur golfer.
  • In 2011, Rory McIlroy became the first player in the history of the game to earn €10 million in career winnings on the European tour.
  • Rory was the top ranked player for a week in 2007 at the age of 17.
  • Rory is a big fan of English Premier League club Manchester United .
  • In 2012 he was honored by the Queen as a Member of the Order of the British Empire for his immense contribution to sport.
  • In 2014, he became the third player, after Jack Nicklaus and Tiger Woods, to have all three major tour wins at the age of 25.
  • Rory McIlroy serves as UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador for Ireland and his first visit in that role was to Haiti in June 2011.
  • In 2012, after winning the PGA Championship, he made history by becoming the first Irish player to reach the top of the professional rankings.
  • In 2012 he almost missed playing the final day of the Ryder Cup by oversleeping and being brought onto the pitch by a police officer just 12 minutes before his starting time.
  • In 2015 he was rewarded with a European Tour Golfer of the Year Award, which was presented for the third time.
