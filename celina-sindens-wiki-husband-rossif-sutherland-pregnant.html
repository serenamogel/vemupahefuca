    Who is Celina Sinden? Wiki Bio

    Celina Sinden was born in London, England on 31 July 1987, so her zodiac sign is Leo and she holds British nationality. She is an actress best known for starring in the “Reign” TV series, while she is also recognized as she’s the wife of Rossif Sutherland, a famous Canadian actor.

    Early life and education

    Celina was raised in London by her mother who is an actress, and her father who was a car salesman. Celina began going to the sets of movies and TV series her mother appeared in at the age of eight, which is when she started being interested in acting.

    She played several sports while at high school such as women soccer, volleyball, and lacrosse, while she also ran track, but taking her mother’s advice, Celina began attending drama classes at her high school, and then enrolled at Guildhall School of Music and Drama, graduating in 2009 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in acting.

    Celina grew up being interested in music as well, while she was also planning on launching a modelling career, but eventually settled with acting.

    Career as an actress and a model

    Celina first launched her career as a model while she was attending Guildhall Drama School, but went on to win her first roles in theatre plays, such as Shakespeare’s “Twelfth Night”, “Nicholas Nickleby” adapted from Dickens’ novel, and “Blood Wedding” by Garcia Lorca, among others.

    She made her debut movie appearance in “My Neighbor’s Dog” short film in 2010 portraying Jane, but didn’t receive much attention for her performance. She only gained recognition after winning the role of Greer Castleroy in the “Reign” drama fantasy TV series in 2013, which follows the story of Mary, Queen of Scots, as she faces political and sexual intrigue in the world of the French court.

    The show aired until 2017 and was a success, while the IMDb website has given it a 7.5/10 score – Celina appeared in all 78 episodes over four years, while some of the other main actresses and actors in the series are Adelaide Kane (78 episodes), Megan Follows (78 episodes), and Torrance Coombs (62 episodes). “Reign” was nominated for 21 awards, winning four such as a Canadian Screen Award for Best Costume Design, a GMS award for best music supervision in a TV drama, and a HPA Award for outstanding color grading – television.

    Love life and marriage with Rossif Sutherland

    Celina’s love life began shortly after she enrolled at Guildhall School of Music and Drama, with another drama student, and were dating for three years after that until graduation, when the two split-up to focus on their careers. Celina went on to meet Rossif Sutherland on the set of the “Reign” TV series in 2013 (Rossif appeared in 20 episodes of the series). They dated for three years before marrying on 21 February 2016, and in the same year she gave birth to their son Theodore Sutherland. Before Celina, Rossif was in a long-term relationship as well, breaking-up a year before he met Celina.

    Hobbies and other interests

    Celina is a huge lover of animals, and has had several pets over the years while she has stated in an interview that she has become pescatarian – a person who doesn’t eat meat but eats fish – to set an example which people might follow. She enjoys watching movies in her spare time, with favorite actor and actress being Johnny Depp and Glenn Close, while the movies she enjoys watching the most are “Alice in Wonderland”, “What’s Eating Gilbert Grape”, and “Guardians of the Galaxy”.

    Celina has travelled a lot throughout the years, and has been around Europe to countries such as Luxemburg, France, and Italy, while she has spent a lot of time in Canada for the shooting of the “Reign” TV series (her husband is also Canadian). She likes spending her free time in nature such as woods, mountains, and close to rivers and lakes, and is planning to live in the countryside in the future growing her own food and taking care of domestic animals.

    Appearance and net worth

    Celina is 32 years old. She has long blonde hair which she occasionally dyes brown, brown eyes, is 5ft 4ins (1.63m) tall, and weighs around 121lbs (55kgs).

    Her net worth is over $500,000, while Rossif’s net worth is over $4 million, as of early 2020.

    Who is Rossif Sutherland, Celina’s husband?

    Rossif Sutherland was born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada on 25 September 1978, his birth sign is Libra and he holds Canadian nationality. He is an actor best known for playing the lead role as Detective Pen Martin in the “King” TV series in 2012. Rossif spent the first seven years of his life in Vancouver, until his family relocated to Paris, France.

    He was raised by highly respected actor Donald Sutherland and actress Francine Racette, both Canadian, with twin sister Rachel, two brothers named Angus and Roeg as well as paternal half-brother Kiefer Sutherland (an actor as well). Rossif attended a high school in Paris, and after matriculation enrolled at Princeton University in New Jersey.

    Rossif made his debut film appearance portraying Fancois Dontelle in “Timeline” in 2003, and went on to appear in many movies such as “Red Doors” in 2005, “Poor Boy’s Game” in 2007, and “The Con Artist” in 2010, which is the movie that made him somewhat famous.

    It is a romantic comedy which follows Vince (Rossif) who, after serving five years in prison, goes back to being a criminal and a car thief. He is also known for portraying Detective Pen Martin in the “King” TV series as well as for playing Nostradamus in the “Reign” TV series, which is when he met his future wife. Some of his latest appearances have been in the “Believe Me: The Abduction of Lisa McVey” film in 2018, and the “Guest of Honour” movie in 2019, while he has been cast to portray Michael in the “Possessor” film set to be released in 2020.
